# Election Results

## 2020 TX-11's open seat after Mike Conaway retired

Republican Primary (10-way field): Pfluger won with 56,093 of 107,435 total votes cast
General election: Pfluger 232,568 — Democrat Jon Mark Hogg 53,394 — Libertarian Wacey Alpha Cody 5,811

## 2022 TX-11's unopposed race

Pfluger was truly unopposed — no opponent of any party. Result: 151,066 to zero

## 2024 TX-11's (mostly) unopposed race

Pfluger ran unopposed by a Democrat, and faced only a Libertarian challenger. Result: 211,975 to 67,637
